# Alpha Scoring Rubric

100-point scale for evaluating alpha candidates. Score each dimension independently, then sum for the composite alpha score.

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Points | What It Measures |
|---|---|---|
| Convergence | 0-30 | Multiple independent signals pointing the same direction. A single signal scores 5-10. Two confirming signals score 15-20. Three or more independent signals converging scores 25-30. Signals must be genuinely independent (e.g., insider buying + technical breakout + options flow), not derivatives of the same data point. |
| Catalyst | 0-25 | Near-term event that triggers repricing. Score higher when the catalyst has a defined date (earnings, FDA decision, index rebalance), the market appears to underestimate the magnitude, and the timing window is weeks not months. A vague "could be big" catalyst scores 5-10. A specific, dated, under-appreciated catalyst scores 20-25. |
| Technical Setup | 0-20 | Price action supports the thesis. Clean breakout from a defined base, volume confirmation, relative strength vs sector, favorable moving average structure. A thesis that fights the chart scores 0-5 regardless of fundamental merit. A thesis aligned with a textbook technical setup scores 15-20. |
| Smart Money | 0-15 | Institutional, insider, or whale activity confirming the thesis. Cluster insider buys (3+ insiders in 30 days) score highest. Single insider transactions score 5-8. Institutional 13F accumulation or on-chain whale wallet activity scores 8-12. No smart money signal scores 0. |
| Asymmetry | 0-10 | Risk/reward skew. How much upside vs downside if the thesis plays out vs fails? A 2:1 R/R scores 3-5. A 3:1+ R/R with defined downside scores 7-10. Undefined downside or poor R/R scores 0-2. |

## Thresholds

| Score Range | Classification | Action |
|---|---|---|
| >= 70 | Strong alpha | High-conviction auto-chain candidate. Full thesis paragraph in Top Pick section. |
| 50-69 | Moderate alpha | Auto-chain eligible. Note caveats -- which dimension is weakest and what would strengthen it. |
| 35-49 | Near-miss | List as watchlist item in the "Nothing Clean" fallback section. Specify the single development that would upgrade it (e.g., "needs insider confirmation" or "waiting for volume breakout"). |
| < 35 | Noise | Do not list. The signal-to-noise ratio is too low. |

## Scoring Integrity

- Score before you rank. Do not adjust scores to fit a desired ranking.
- A candidate with a perfect catalyst (25/25) but no convergence (5/30) and no smart money (0/15) is a 40-50, not a top pick. Convergence is the heaviest dimension for a reason.
- When data is missing for a dimension, score conservatively (bottom third of the range). Do not assume favorable conditions.
